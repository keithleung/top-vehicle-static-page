The sotrage service at Top Vehicle Management is second to none and has been conceived to offer vehicle owners and collectors total peace of mind.

Upon arrival, each vehicle will be subject to a care ful multi-stage safe wash by our would renowned on-site detailing partner, Elite Detailing and Protection (EPD). Acomprehensive photographic inspection report will follow within 24 hours, during which time the vehicle will be left to air dry. If there are accessories accompanying be documented and photographed to form an inventory list.

Final pre-storage preparation includes a check over of engine fluids, battery condition and adjustment of tire pressures.

Once a vehicle has passed through the pre-storage process, it is accepted into our climate controlled storage facility with a target of 50-55% relative humidity maintained throughout the year. In addition to 24 hour CCTV coverage within the storage area, the entrance to the facility is situated above ground and also benefits from round the clock security personnel on guard.

Top Vehicle Management employs a professional team on site that prides itself on a proactive management approach. All vehicles under our care benefit from the following interval services:

  • Daily – visual inspection by a member of our on-site team
  • Every 14 days – battery conditioned and checked, drip tray inspected, fluids and pressures checked
  • Every month – engine started, vehicle run up to temperature. Any defects, issues or warnings noted and notified to client in a report with suggested remedies.

With 48 hours of advance notice, our team can assist with readying your vehicle for delivery or simply for drive. Clients have exclusive use of our lounge area adjacent to our dedicated hand over bays, where pick up or drop off can be arranged 24/7.
